George Washington

1732-1799

George Washington Was A Founding Father And The First President Of The United States, Serving From 1789 to 1797. As Commander Of The Continental Army, Washington Led Patriot Forces To Victory In The American Revolutionary War Against The British Empire.

George Washington

The following list is about Life of George Washington :

  • George Washington Was Born On February 22, 1732, In Westmoreland County, Virginia.
  • He Worked As A Surveyor In His Early Years And Gained Experience In Land Measurement And Exploration.
  • Washington Served As A Military Officer During The French And Indian War.
  • He Was Appointed As The Commander-In-Chief Of The Continental Army During The American Revolutionary War In 1775.
  • He Led The American Forces To Victory Against The British And Played A Key Role In Securing American Independence In 1783.
  • Washington Presided Over The Constitutional Convention In 1787, Which Led To The Drafting Of The United States Constitution.
  • He Became The First President Of The United States In 1789 And Served Two Terms Until 1797.
  • He Voluntarily Stepped Down After Two Terms, Setting A Precedent For Future U.S. Presidents.
  • George Washington Died On December 14, 1799, At His Plantation, Mount Vernon, Virginia.
