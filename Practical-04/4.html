Vision

To foster an environment that empowers people, organizations and society through education, ideas, research and training.

Mission

  • To provide quality education and thereby bring social transformation.
  • To create leaders through innovation and entrepreneurship.

Program Specific Outcome

  1. Ability to adapt to dynamic environments with a solid understanding of societal, professional, and ecological issues.
  2. Capability to function in a multi-disciplinary work environment as a team leader with strong interpersonal skills, appreciating professional ethics.
  3. Effective utilization of technology in business, supported by analytical, research-based training and 3-layer learning.
